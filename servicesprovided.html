Here is what we can deliver to you:-

1)PCN: (Project concept Note) After study of the project objective and the operating plant parameters, a basic concept note is prepared to enable the prospective developers in taking the fundamental decision on investment and type and basic configuration of the project.

2)PFR: (Project feasibility Report) A few initial designs are developed comprising plant process flow diagram (PFD) and initial financial analysis is carried out to determine the financial viability of the project for different scenarios/ configurations & development of business model for benefit of stake holders, investors, bankers etc.

3)DPR: (Detail Project Report) Detailed exercise is carried out comprising:
- Requirement of Land, water, Fuel linkage/sourcing, Estimation of Tariff, requirement of Statutory & Non Statutory clearances for the project.
- Site survey
- Basic engineering of the project including broad procurement specifications
- Project costing
- Financial and economic analysis
- Risk analysis
- Environmental analysis
Detailed project report is prepared as per norms of the financial institutions being considered for raising loan for the project debt component. Necessary support is also provided for carrying out negotiations with the Banks/FIs.

4)ES: (Engineering support)
The scope of review/ Re-engineering services depends upon the project implementation methodology preferred by the project developer. In case of EPC contracted project, such services generally cover vetting of the engineering done by the individual vendors and detailing of the tie-in points for the rest.
Construction supervisory and project management services are also provided; the exhaustiveness is negotiated as per the requirement of the project developer.

5)PS: (Procurement support)
Preparation of the procurement documentation including tenders, evaluation of offers and assistance in carrying out negotiations before placement of purchase orders are covered under this service. Post order vendor follow up and quality management services are also provided.

6)IS: (Inspection Support)
Carryout stage and quality inspection services at vendor works.

7)OMS: (Operation & Maintenance support)
The scope of operation and maintenance services will include review of O&M manual, spare part list and determination of adequacy based on equipment overall and running hours. Review the Transition plan and support during Transition between O&M contractor stepping- in and EPC & Non EPC contractor stepping out.

8)Power Transmission & Distribution:
Power Transmission & Distribution sector in Procurement, Project Engineering functions for system like EHV substations, HVDS, Equipment and auxiliaries associated with Power Distribution, Network Automation, SCADA & Distribution management system (DMS).
